Who are we?

The team consists of passionate members ranging in age from 12 to 18, and the team is divided into several internal sub-teams, each team has specific tasks that seeks to accomplish.

Why the team was established: Quanta Team was founded with the vision of building a generation capable of creating a harmonious environment between academics and specialists in different industries and creating use cases for advanced technologies in the field of robotics (programming, control engineering Communications, artificial intelligence, quantum computing, etc.).

What distinguishes us from others: We seek to integrate the skills in technology with the business side, so that the team members will be able in the future to transform their skills into private businesses, as we build a bridge to cross the skills gap between students of the stage preparatory & secondary, and the future needs in advanced technology.
